Our Story



MukVeg was founded on the 2nd of August 2021 by 4 young women with a mission, an idea and a single thought. They were thinking about the future of food. Grace, Emilija, Selay and Dev are passionate about the environment and believe that by implementing more vegetarian and vegan meals into people's diet overall health will improve and there will be a reduction of greenhouse gas emissions.

The story behind our name

Our name MukVeg is inspired by the trend called Mukbang. They are videos, especially ones that is live-streamed, that feature a person eating a large quantity of food and addressing an audience. The word mukbang (먹방; meokbang) is a combination of the Korean words for "eating" (먹는; meongneun) and "broadcast" (방송; bangsong). So our brand name translates to "eat veg" which, is what we promote here.
